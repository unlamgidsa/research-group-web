Groundstation

May 26, 2021

First step to close the cycle Engineering, from satellite to final applicacion. This week we had a success test of the new antenna. It was build in Argentina and works well with a Mini 70 amplifier. Using gpredict, gqrx and GNU Radio (helped by gr-satellites) we downloaded the bugsat1 (TITA) beacon directly from satellite. Finally we decoded audio successfully. This is our first step to complete the cycle; from the satellite to final application, Unlam Ground Segment.
We also planing to create a SatNogs ground station soon!.
